Biryani

Description

Biryani is a fragrant Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, meat or vegetables, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or meat
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 3 green cardamom pods
  • 2 black cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil

Steps

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then boil until 70% cooked.
  2. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger, garlic, and spices for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee and fry onions until golden brown.
  4. In a heavy pot, layer marinated meat and partially cooked rice alternately.
  5. Cover the pot and cook on high heat for 3-4 minutes, then low heat for 45 minutes.
  6. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
