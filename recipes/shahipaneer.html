Shahi Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a creamy and aromatic North Indian curry made with cottage cheese (paneer) in a rich tomato-based sauce with cream and spices.

Ingredients

  • 400g paneer, cubed
  • 2 cups tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger and garlic, cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add garam masala, chili powder, and salt.
  5. Stir in cream and bring to a simmer.
  6. Gently add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
